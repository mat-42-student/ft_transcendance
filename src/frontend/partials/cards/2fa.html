Enable Two-Factor Authentication


Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security. After enabling 2FA, you’ll need a Time-based One-Time Password (TOTP) from an authenticator app (e.g., Google Authenticator, Authy, Microsoft Authenticator) when you log in.


  1. Click Generate QR Code to get your unique setup QR.

  2. Open your authenticator app and scan the QR code.

  3. Enter the TOTP code displayed in your app to finish verification.
